Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Play Tube Team handles your information when you use the Play Tube mobile application.

Effective Date: July 4, 2026 Contact: monsifelmoudden3030@gmail.com

1. Information We Process

To provide a functional media playback experience, the app may process basic technical data and in-app preferences. This includes:

  • Locally stored settings, playback history, and theme preferences.
  • Basic technical properties (such as device type, OS version, and language configuration).
  • Temporary media cache or downloaded metadata necessary for handling offline features.

2. Device Permissions

We respect your device resources. The application is built without access to sensitive tracking capabilities:

  • No Location Tracking: The app does not request or use coarse or fine location permissions (e.g., android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION).
  • No Haptic Overuse: The app does not declare or trigger vibration features (android.permission.VIBRATE).

3. How Information Is Used

Any technical configuration data processed by the application is solely utilized to:

  • Deliver fluid media playback, streaming feeds, and user-initiated navigation features.
  • Retain user choices such as playlists, internal favorites, and application layouts.
  • Diagnose local runtime errors, application crashes, or system performance conflicts.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may communicate with external content networks, hosting platforms, or media API endpoints to load online feeds. These external infrastructures operate under their respective independent privacy frameworks. We do not sell or rent user information to third-party brokers.

5. Data Retention & User Controls

Most interaction records (such as playlist builds and history counters) stay isolated on your local storage. You maintain complete control over this data:

  • You can clear application caches and data directly through your Android device settings.
  • Uninstalling the mobile application permanently removes all corresponding localized data stores from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly request or gather personally identifiable tracking parameters from individuals under the legal age threshold. If you discover that a minor has submitted unauthorized data channels, please notify us immediately so we can apply corrections.
